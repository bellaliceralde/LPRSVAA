Turn-Based Battle Game - The Zone

Rules and Regulations

  • The game starts by either the player or the enemy going first. This is decided through a coin flip.
  • Whoever wins the coin flip deals damage to the opposing side first, while the side that receives the damage cannot deal damage back for the meantime.
  • There are potions you can use while playing:
    • Healing Potion: Adds 30+ health to your current health. Max health of 100. Can be used 3 times. Can be used by both the enemy and the player.
    • Immunity Potion: Become immune to the next attack by the enemy. Can be used 2 times. Can only be used by the player.
    • Strength Potion: Next attack deals 2x damage. Can only be used once. Can only be used by the player.
  • Damage dealt by both the player and the enemy ranges from 2 to 10.
  • The enemy has a 10% chance to deal critical damage to the player.
  • Attacks that are blocked only deal 2 to 3 damage.
  • If both parties decide to block, no damage will be inflicted on either side.
  • The game ends when either the player's health or the enemy's health hits zero.
